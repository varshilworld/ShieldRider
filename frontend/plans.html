How It Works

ShieldRider dynamically adjusts insurance protection using real-time environmental signals. Instead of waiting for manual claims, automatic payouts trigger when conditions breach thresholds.

🌧️ Heavy Rain

Rainfall > 50mm → Auto payout ₹500–₹1000

🌡️ Extreme Heat

Temperature > 45°C → Heatwave compensation ₹300–₹600

💨 AQI Pollution

AQI > 200 → Pollution protection ₹400–₹800

⚠️ Storm Alert

Wind > 60 km/h → Emergency payout ₹1000–₹2000

Future Integration:

ShieldRider will integrate with OpenWeatherMap API and India Meteorological Department feeds to automate this process. No more manual claims — just real protection when you need it.
